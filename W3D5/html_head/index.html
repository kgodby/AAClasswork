Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tomato, thinly sliced
  • 3 slices turkey bacon
  • 3 slices Cheddar cheese
  • 3 lettuce leaves

Triple Decker Turkey Bacon Sandwich

Descriptions:

Layers of turkey bacon, Cheddar cheese, lettuce, tomato, and mayo grace two slices of bread for this triple decker doozie. A wonderful sandwich at any time of the day.

Directions

  1. Toast the bread slices.
  2. Spread a thin layer of mayonnaise on each slice.
  3. Layer bottom slice with lettuce, tomato, turkey bacon, and cheese.
  4. Spread another thin layer of mayonnaise on the cheese; repeat layers twice.
  5. Top with second slice of bread.
  6. Heat in microwave for 45 seconds to 1 minute, or until cheese is melted.
